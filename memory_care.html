MEMORY CARE
FIND PEACE OF MIND WITH A CONTINUUM OF SENIOR CARE

GCC Centre is a continuing care retirement community that offers a full continuum of senior health services. In addition to assisted living and memory care, we also provide skilled nursing and rehabilitation services. No matter what level of care your mom or dad requires, you’ll have peace of mind knowing our dedicated and compassionate team of caregivers, therapists, and medical professionals will support them every step of the way in a safe, comfortable atmosphere..

PERSONALIZED, THOUGHTFUL AND INNOVATIVE MEMORY CARE PROGRAMS

At GCC Centre, we work with individuals, family members, and caregivers, searching for the right balance of respect and care. GCC Centre’s staff deliver compassionate dementia care based on these innovative, evidence-based programs:

Landmarks for Living® emphasizes each Resident’s life experiences and helps them retain and restore their independence. This program is nationally recognized for excellence in serving people who need care for Alzheimer’s or other age-related dementias.

PARO Pets is based on research that interaction with pets, including robotic animals, can improve the psychological health of those with dementia. By interacting and playing with our automated PARO pet seals, Benedetto “Bene” and Bella, Residents reap the benefits of pet therapy.

MUSIC & MEMORY® is a memory care program that features innovative techniques based on the latest neuroscience research about how our brains respond to music. This program uses personalized digital music therapy to trigger memories and improve quality of life.

Our dementia-specific programs are led by specially trained and compassionate staff who promote confidence, provide comfort and encourage residents to build meaningful social connections. While residents receive priority access, memory care at GCC Centre is open to all seniors affected by Alzheimer’s disease and other forms of dementia.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I know when a loved one needs memory care?

    Memory loss, whether from Alzheimer’s or other forms of dementia, affects more than just one person. At GCC Centre, we work with individuals, family members and caregivers, searching for the right balance of respect and care.

    Watch for behavior or lifestyle changes, and when help is clearly needed, go with your loved one for a professional evaluation.

    • - Problems with walking, talking, eating, dressing or managing medications
    • - Wandering
    • - Loss of short-term memory and diminished reasoning and task completion skills
    • - Uncharacteristic appearance and personal hygiene
    • - Incontinence
    • - Changes in appetite or sleep patterns
    • - Depression, withdrawal, abuse of alcohol or other drugs
  • What is the difference between Alzheimer's disease and dementia?.

    It’s best to start with a physician’s assessment. Depending on the circumstances of your situation, or that of your loved one, you can match needs to the right level of assistance. When help with daily tasks is needed — taking medicines, bathing, dressing, meals, transportation and housekeeping — assisted living can be the answer. Trained professionals provide support while helping preserve an individual’s independence.

  • How are your memory care staff special?

    We’re proud of our long-serving memory care staff, for their dedicated expertise in this specialized area, and for their genuine compassion in working with Residents affected by memory loss. They have the gift of being person-centered in their interactions and bring exceptional patience, gentleness and a passion for consistently providing dignified and respectful care to our Memory Care Residents and their families. They’re fully trained in the Landmarks for Living philosophies, PARO Pet Therapy, and MUSIC & MEMORY ® programs.
