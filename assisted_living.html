ASSISTED LIVING

We’re here to lend a hand when daily tasks become more challenging.

CARE DELIVERED IN THE COMFORT OF YOUR HOME

Dignified and professional care is delivered in the comfort and privacy of your GCC Centre apartment home. Residences in assisted living are maintenance-free and include kitchenettes, hallways, and bathrooms with enhanced safety measures to help you maintain your independence. Caregivers are on duty 24/7, and each senior apartment home includes an emergency call system to ensure help is always available when you need it.

COMPASSIONATE AND TRUSTWORTHY PERSONAL CAREGIVERS

Our goal is to help you live as independently as possible with compassionate assisted living services delivered exactly when you need them. Your personal caregiver may come to your home for just a couple of hours, a few days of the week, or more frequently. We’ll customize a schedule according to your unique needs and preferences, keeping you in control of how you wish to be helped. Whether it’s helping you get dressed, assisting with your medications, or scheduling transportation for medical appointments, no task is too big or too small for our dedicated and trustworthy team members.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is assisted living?

    Assisted living provides a touch of help for those who need it, with a distinct combination of individualized care, familiar neighborhood surroundings and professional expertise. Our residents are assured ongoing wellness evaluations and levels of care that address their specific needs. Licensed, compassionate staff assists with daily tasks as needed, and the many amenities available in assisted living help these residents more easily enjoy what matters most to them.

  • How do I know when somenone needs assisted living?

    It’s best to start with a physician’s assessment. Depending on the circumstances of your situation, or that of your loved one, you can match needs to the right level of assistance. When help with daily tasks is needed — taking medicines, bathing, dressing, meals, transportation and housekeeping — assisted living can be the answer. Trained professionals provide support while helping preserve an individual’s independence.

  • What if the need for daily help is more than anticipated?

    At GCC Centre, we offer a continuum of care. In addition to assisted living, we provide memory care and skilled nursing. These programs provide round-the-clock care for individuals recovering from illness or injury, or those needing more long-term supervision or support.
